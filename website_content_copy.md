### 5. FAQ (Operational Details)
* **Q**: Are roadside shelters / pickup sheds included?
  * **A**: Roadside shelters (pickup sheds) are under the PWD Department and are not included in this advertising inventory. Media coverage is limited to MSRTC bus stations, depots, and buses.
* **Q**: Are all depots equally available?
  * **A**: Some depots are under BOT (Build-Operate-Transfer) or have BOT history. Availability and conditions are determined depot-wise.
* **Q**: How fast can a campaign go live?
  * **A**: Digital campaigns can go live quickly depending on media availability and approval. Physical media can go live in 48–72 hours after approval, printing, and mounting.
* **Q**: What proof do you provide for campaigns?
  * **A**: GPS-enabled start photos, GPS-enabled end photos, installation proof, digital screen logs, display count access where applicable, campaign execution reports, and depot-wise visibility proof.
